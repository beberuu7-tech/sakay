About Sakay Transportation

Your Trusted Booking Companion since 2025

Who We Are

Sakay is a modern student transportation booking system designed to make daily commutes safe, reliable, and convenient for students across Biliran Province. We connect students from Culaba, Kawayan, and Almeria municipalities to Naval, the provincial capital.

Our platform revolutionizes student transportation by offering features like real-time tracking, secure online payments, and flexible scheduling options. We understand the importance of safe and reliable transportation for students, and we're committed to providing the best service possible.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable transportation services for students in Biliran Province, ensuring every student can access education without transportation barriers.

Our Vision

To become the leading student transportation service in Eastern Visayas, known for safety, reliability, and innovative technology solutions.

Our Core Values

Safety First

Student safety is our top priority. All vehicles and drivers are thoroughly vetted.

Reliability

Consistent, on-time service you can count on every single day.

Integrity

Honest, transparent operations with fair pricing and ethical practices.

Innovation

Leveraging technology to improve the transportation experience.

Community

Supporting local communities and helping students access education.

Sustainability

Committed to eco-friendly practices and reducing our carbon footprint.

What We Offer

Regular Routes: Daily transportation from Culaba, Kawayan, and Almeria to Naval with multiple pickup and drop-off points.

Easy Booking: Book your trips online with our user-friendly booking system. No more waiting at uncertain pick-up points.

Flexible Payment: Multiple payment options including cash, GCash, and card payments for your convenience.

Real-Time Tracking: Know exactly where your vehicle is and when it will arrive at your pickup point.

Safety Assured: All drivers undergo background checks, vehicles are regularly maintained, and we have 24/7 customer support.

Parent Notifications: Keep parents informed with automatic notifications about pickup, drop-off, and trip status.

Our Coverage

From: Culaba, Kawayan, Almeria
To: Naval (Provincial Capital)
Service Hours: Morning (5:00 AM - 8:30 AM) and Afternoon (3:00 PM - 7:30 PM)
Operating Days: Monday to Saturday
